About

Sci-Grooves is bonded by a common thread - the realm of dark electronic music. Here, our love for the shadowy sonic spectrum becomes the driving force that binds us. Navigating this dark world of electronic music, we strive to create innovative experiences that showcase the diversity and expressiveness of the genre.

Embracing the mysterious, atmospheric, experimental and nocturnal sound, we invite listeners on a journey beyond the usual boundaries of sound. With each note, we aim to convey our dedication and passion for electronic sound. Welcome to Sci-Grooves, where the dark magic of music comes alive.

We are driven by a shared vision that transcends the speed or style of music, our focus lies in broadening the perspective of dark electronic music. We invite you to join us on this immersive sonic exploration.

Label Artists

  • Meander

    Behind the production of Meander from Athens (Gr), is Michael Fourlas. He discovered the temple of electronic music at the middle of 90’s and he became addicted, so at the year of 2002 he starts studying audio engineering and music technology. At 2005 he started to play powerful Dj set and organize events. After since years of playing in several dancefloors and several studies in sound, it was a question of time to start producing his own music. Powerful grooves with lots of spicy psychedelic elements, compose a deep atmosphere thus creating his unique style under dark progressive genre. The first track from Meander was released in 2008 and since then he has an active presence in many well-known and established labels. Watch out and stay tuned for more music in the future!

  • Taz

    T.A.Z. – Temporary Autonom Zone Ben Melech, born under the October sky of 1985, found his calling early — drawn by the hypnotic pulse of psychedelic trance echoing through his circle of friends. At sixteen, he stepped into his first rave — deep in the heart of nature, where the forest whispered, the hills breathed, and the desert dreamed. From that moment, the rhythm claimed him. By nineteen, he was behind the decks, weaving frequencies into journeys — deep, organic, and powerful psychedelic tales pulsing between 156 and 160+ BPM. Soon curiosity led him into the world of Cubase, where sound became alchemy and every vibration a brushstroke on the canvas of consciousness. After countless nights of experimentation and flow, his tracks began to take shape — alive, breathing, moving. When he finally shared them under the open sky, the dancefloors responded — bodies in motion, spirits awake, united in a Temporary Autonom Zone of pure energy and freedom.

  • Youtopia

    Behind the project Youtopia, is Chrisa Efstathiou from Athens (Greece). Her love and her passion for electronic music began in her school years, with a special peak in her student years (2019), where she also made the decision to study music technology and work with the production of her own music. Infused with ethereal elements, her music creates profound and melancholic atmospheres, embodying a distinct dark ambiance. Playful melodies intertwine seamlessly with dynamic lead elements, all underscored by haunting percussions. Through a sonic journey, her creations encapsulate a mysterious allure, drawing the listener into a world of shadowy depth and emotive intensity.
