About

Sci-Grooves is bonded by a common thread - the realm of dark electronic music. Here, our love for the shadowy sonic spectrum becomes the driving force that binds us. Navigating this dark world of electronic music, we strive to create innovative experiences that showcase the diversity and expressiveness of the genre.

Embracing the mysterious, atmospheric, experimental and nocturnal sound, we invite listeners on a journey beyond the usual boundaries of sound. With each note, we aim to convey our dedication and passion for electronic sound. Welcome to Sci-Grooves, where the dark magic of music comes alive.

We are driven by a shared vision that transcends the speed or style of music, our focus lies in broadening the perspective of dark electronic music. We invite you to join us on this immersive sonic exploration.
